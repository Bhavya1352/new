Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get coverage?

Most policies are activated within 24 hours of approval. Some plans offer instant coverage upon payment confirmation.

What documents do I need to apply?

You'll need a valid ID, proof of address, and basic health information. Our team will guide you through the entire process.

Can I cancel my policy anytime?

Yes, you can cancel anytime. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not completely satisfied.

How do I file a claim?

File claims online through your dashboard, mobile app, or call our 24/7 helpline. Average claim settlement time is 24-48 hours.

Insurance Knowledge Hub

Practical guidance to help families choose smarter coverage, avoid common mistakes, and reduce claim delays.

Life Cover Planning

Use the 10x income rule as a baseline, then adjust for loans, education goals, and monthly expenses.

  • Account for inflation over 15-20 years
  • Keep nominee details updated yearly
  • Review after major life events

Health Policy Optimization

Pick a base plan plus top-up for cost efficiency instead of buying one expensive slab directly.

  • Check room-rent limits and co-pay
  • Compare day-care procedure coverage
  • Confirm waiting period clauses

Home Risk Checklist

Most claims fail due to documentation gaps. Keep purchase bills and asset photos safely archived.

  • Store digital invoice copies in cloud
  • Record yearly property condition photos
  • Update high-value items list quarterly

Claim Readiness Score

Our advisors can pre-audit your policy documents so you can file claims with fewer back-and-forths.

  • ID and address proof verified
  • Medical records folder prepared
  • Emergency contact mapped to policy

Annual Coverage Review Program

Every policy year, our team runs a benefit-gap review to check whether your current coverage still matches your family responsibilities and financial goals.

This includes premium efficiency analysis, rider relevance checks, and claim-experience benchmarking against industry averages.
